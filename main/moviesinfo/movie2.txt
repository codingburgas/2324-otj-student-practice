			The Godfather is a cinematic masterpiece.
			It was released in 1972. Directed by Francis Ford Coppola.
			Based on the novel by Mario Puzo. The story follows the Corleone crime family.
			It stars Marlon Brando as Vito Corleone. Also features Al Pacino as Michael Corleone.
			The movie depicts the gritty reality of the Mafia. It is considered one of the greatest films ever made.
