			Forrest Gump is a beloved and heartwarming film.
			It was released in 1994. Directed by Robert Zemeckis.
			Based on the novel by Winston Groom. The story follows the life of Forrest Gump, played by Tom Hanks.
			It spans several decades of American history. Forrest witnesses and influences many major events.
			The movie is a mix of comedy and drama. It explores themes of love, loss, and the importance of family and friendship.
			Forrest Gump was a critical and commercial success. It won multiple Academy Awards, including Best Picture.
