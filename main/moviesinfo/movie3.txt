			The Dark Knight is a critically acclaimed superhero film.
			It was released in 2008. Directed by Christopher Nolan.
			Based on the iconic Batman character. The story follows Bruce Wayne's quest to protect Gotham City.
			It stars Christian Bale as Batman. Features an unforgettable performance by Heath Ledger as the Joker.
			The movie explores themes of chaos and morality. It is widely regarded as one of the best superhero movies ever made.
