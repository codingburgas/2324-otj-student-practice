			Schindler's List is a powerful and emotional historical drama.
			It was released in 1993. Directed by Steven Spielberg.
			Based on the true story of Oskar Schindler. He was a German businessman who saved Jewish lives during WWII.
			The movie stars Liam Neeson as Schindler. It depicts the horrors of the Holocaust in a stark and haunting way.
			It is a poignant tale of courage and humanity. The film won numerous awards, including Best Picture at the Oscars.
