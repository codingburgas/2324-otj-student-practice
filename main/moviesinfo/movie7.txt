			Inception is a mind-bending science fiction film.
			It was released in 2010. Directed by Christopher Nolan.
			The story revolves around a skilled thief named Dom Cobb, played by Leonardo DiCaprio.
			Cobb and his team specialize in corporate espionage. They infiltrate people's dreams to steal valuable information.
			The movie explores the concept of dream-sharing technology. It features complex storylines within multiple dream levels.
			Inception is praised for its originality and visually stunning effects. It blurs the lines between reality and dreams in a captivating way.
			The film was a critical and commercial success. It is considered one of Nolan's most ambitious and innovative works.
