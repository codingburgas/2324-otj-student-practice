				The Shawshank Redemption is a classic film.
				It was released in 1994. Directed by Frank Darabont.
				Based on a Stephen King novella. The story follows
				Andy Dufresne. He is wrongly convicted of murder.
				Andy befriends fellow inmate Red. Together, they navigate prison life.
				The movie is known for its twist. It has a powerful message of hope.
